This graph shows the distribution of passenger class. Even though Titanic was infamous as one of the most luxurious ships back at its day, it actually made a lot of its earnings carrying poor immigrants from Europe to the US. The rooms and accomodation that these people had (3rd class passengers) were much less luxurious and often lacking. Data shows that there is a much larger proportion of 3rd class passengers compared to other classes, but 1st and 2nd class passenger numbers were similar.
